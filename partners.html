Collaboration Across Continents

ETS Collaboration

As part of the FRQGAN4AD project, École de Technologie Supérieure (ETS) in Canada contributes significantly with its advanced tool suites. Under the guidance of Prof. Rami Langar, ETS will provide access to the Kirq Quantum Communication test bed (https://kirq.numana.tech/en/) offered by Numana.

Innovation and Impact

This collaboration between ETS and the EU partner aims to foster innovation, develop commercial opportunities, and attract global telecom sector stakeholders and industries focused on future 6G technologies. By leveraging complementary expertise, this partnership will pioneer cross-Atlantic experimental setups and create a robust framework for network anomaly detection.

For more information, contact us at esposito@unisa.it or rami.langar@etsmtl.ca.
