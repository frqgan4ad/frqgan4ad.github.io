Collaboration Across Continents

University of Salerno (UNISA)

Located in Fisciano, Italy, the University of Salerno (UNISA) is a prominent academic institution known for its commitment to excellence in education and research. UNISA offers a wide range of programs and has a strong focus on fostering innovation and collaboration across various disciplines.

École de Technologie Supérieure (ÉTS)

École de Technologie Supérieure (ÉTS), situated in Montreal, Canada, specializes in engineering and technology education. Under the leadership of Prof. Rami Langar, ÉTS contributes significantly to the FRQGAN4AD project by providing access to advanced quantum communication test beds, facilitating cutting-edge research and development.

Numana

Numana is a catalyst for the technological ecosystem in Quebec, focusing on emerging technologies and disruptive models that will shape the future. By identifying and promoting innovative technologies, Numana plays a crucial role in fostering collaboration between academia and industry, thereby contributing to the advancement of the FRQGAN4AD project's objectives.

Innovation and Impact

This collaboration aims to foster innovation, develop commercial opportunities, and attract global telecom sector stakeholders and industries focused on future 6G technologies. By leveraging complementary expertise, this partnership will pioneer cross-Atlantic experimental setups and create a robust framework for network anomaly detection.
